# Privacy Policy - DPDC Balance Checker

**Last updated:** June 2026

DPDC Balance Checker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- Your customer number is stored **locally on your device only**.
- Balance data is fetched directly from the DPDC API and stored **locally on your device only**.
- No analytics, no tracking, no account required.
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.
